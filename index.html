Rumors, Fake News, Disinformation, Misinformation


Rumors & Misinformation

Rumors and disinformation refer to false or misleading information spread through communication channels. While rumors can be harmless, disinformation can have serious consequences, such as manipulating public opinion or causing panic. It is important to verify information and rely on trustworthy sources to prevent harm.

Fake News & Disinformation

Fake news and misinformation are false or misleading information that are deliberately spread through various channels, such as social media or propaganda. They can be used to manipulate public opinion or create confusion. It is crucial to verify information before sharing and rely on reliable sources to prevent the harmful effects of fake news and misinformation.
