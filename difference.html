Comparison between Rumors and Misinformation

Rumor Misinformation
Uncertain or unverified information Incorrect or inaccurate information
Can be either malicious or benign Can arise from errors, misunderstandings, or misinterpretation
Can sometimes turn out to be true Does not imply intent to deceive

Comparison between Fake News and Disinformation

Fake New Disinformation
News that contains false information False or misleading information
Presented as if it were real news Spread intentionally to deceive
Often associated with non-traditional news sources, like social media or certain website Can take many forms, not just news
Intent can vary, from deception to generating web traffic and ad revenue Often used in a political context
